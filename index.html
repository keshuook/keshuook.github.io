About Me

Hi, I'm Kailash Prasad, an undergraduate student at Krea University (Class of 2029). Prior to this, I studied at Abacus Montessori School in Chennai. My school gave me the freedom to explore any topic that interested me without teachers forcing their agenda upon me.
Currently, my main interests are programming and electronics. I began programming with Scratch and later taught myself JavaScript, Java, and Python. I have made many games and tools using these languages. You can find some of them on this website.
My interest in electronics began when I first learned the basic principles of electricity and applied them to small projects, like a water level indicator and logic gates. This interest deepened when I discovered microcontrollers, after getting a BBC micro:bit. Then, I experimented with Arduinos, ESP boards and Raspberry Pi Picos. Outside of tech, I enjoy music. I sing and play the guitar, and I enjoy performing for friends and small audiences.
