IMPORTANT: The 1st level of this game needs a camera. If you don't have one, you can skip the level by answering NO to the question. This game has 3 levels and 1 celebration level. At every level, you can hear the instructions. You can also see it if you press the instructions button. FIRST LEVEL RULES Use any part of your body to save the watermelons. If a watermelon falls in lava you lose life. SECOND LEVEL RULES Use the left arrow and right arrow to dodge the watermelons. You win if your score is 10. You have 5 lives. THIRD LEVEL RULES Use the left arrow and right arrow to catch watermelons. If your time runs out you lose. If you get a score of 10 you win. PARTY LEVEL Pop the balloons with your mouse and see what happens
