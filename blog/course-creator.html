An AI-based course creator

Published on June 25, 2025 by Kailash Prasad

This video showcases an AI-based course maker I created. I got this idea after watching my mother create courses. First, she writes a storyboard with details of the course, and then uses (expensive) software to develop it into a course. I thought to myself, what if I could completely automate the development process for free? So here we are.
